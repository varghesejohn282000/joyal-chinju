Wedding Ceremony

11:30 AM

St. Mary's Church, Vazhavara
Kerala

The sacred wedding ceremony, celebrated according to the holy traditions of the Kerala Syrian Orthodox Church. The ceremony begins with the Modhiravazhvu Susrusha (Exchange of Rings), followed by the solemn Holy Crowning Ceremony — the tying of the Minnu, placing of the Manthrakodi, and priestly blessings that sanctify the union of Chinju and Joyal.
